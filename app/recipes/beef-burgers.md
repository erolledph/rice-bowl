# Beef Burgers

Classic homemade beef burgers with fresh toppings and melted cheese.

## Ingredients

- 800g ground beef
- 4 burger buns
- 4 slices cheddar cheese
- Lettuce leaves
- Tomato slices
- Onion slices
- Pickles
- Mayonnaise
- Ketchup
- Mustard
- Salt and pepper

## Instructions

1. Form beef into 4 patties, season with salt and pepper
2. Heat grill or pan to medium-high
3. Cook burgers for 3-4 minutes per side
4. Add cheese in the last minute and let melt
5. Toast burger buns
6. Spread condiments on buns
7. Place burger on bottom bun
8. Add lettuce, tomato, onion, and pickles
9. Top with bun and serve
