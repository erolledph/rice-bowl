# Chicken Tikka Masala

Tender marinated chicken cooked in a creamy tomato-based curry sauce.

## Ingredients

- 800g chicken breast, cubed
- 200ml yogurt
- 400ml coconut milk
- 400g canned tomatoes
- 3 tbsp tikka masala paste
- 2 onions, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp ginger, minced
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- Fresh cilantro
- Salt and pepper

## Instructions

1. Marinate chicken in yogurt and half the paste for 30 minutes
2. Heat oil and cook marinated chicken until golden, set aside
3. Saut√© onions, garlic, and ginger until softened
4. Add remaining paste and cook for 2 minutes
5. Add canned tomatoes and coconut milk
6. Return chicken to pan and simmer for 15 minutes
7. Season with salt and pepper, garnish with cilantro
8. Serve with rice or naan
