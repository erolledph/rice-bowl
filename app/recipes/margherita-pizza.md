# Margherita Pizza

Classic Italian pizza with simple yet flavorful toppings.

## Ingredients

- 1 pre-made pizza dough
- 200g fresh mozzarella
- 2 tbsp tomato sauce
- Fresh basil leaves
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Salt and pepper
- Oregano

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 220Â°C
2. Roll out pizza dough on a baking sheet
3. Spread tomato sauce evenly
4. Tear mozzarella and distribute over sauce
5. Drizzle with olive oil
6. Bake for 12-15 minutes until crust is golden
7. Remove from oven, add fresh basil
8. Season with salt, pepper, and oregano
9. Slice and serve hot
