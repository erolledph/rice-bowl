# Shrimp Scampi

Elegant and quick shrimp dish with a delicious garlic-lemon sauce.

## Ingredients

- 600g large shrimp, peeled
- 4 tbsp butter
- 6 cloves garlic, minced
- 150ml white wine
- Juice of 2 lemons
- 400g pasta
- Red pepper flakes
- Fresh parsley
- Salt and pepper
- Olive oil

## Instructions

1. Cook pasta according to package directions
2. Heat olive oil and butter in a large pan
3. Add garlic and saut√© for 1 minute
4. Add shrimp and cook until pink (3-4 minutes)
5. Deglaze pan with white wine
6. Add lemon juice and simmer for 2 minutes
7. Season with salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes
8. Drain pasta and toss with shrimp and sauce
9. Garnish with fresh parsley
10. Serve immediately
