# Fish and Chips

Classic British dish with crispy battered fish and golden fries.

## Ingredients

- 2 fish fillets (200g each)
- 4 medium potatoes
- 1 cup flour
- 250ml beer or sparkling water
- 1 tsp baking powder
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- Salt and pepper
- Vinegar for serving
- Oil for frying

## Instructions

1. Cut potatoes into chips and soak in water
2. Heat oil to 180Â°C and fry chips until golden, drain and salt
3. Whisk flour, baking powder, beer, garlic, salt, and pepper for batter
4. Pat fish dry and coat with flour
5. Dip in batter and deep fry until golden (4-5 minutes)
6. Drain on paper towels
7. Serve with chips, vinegar, and tartare sauce
