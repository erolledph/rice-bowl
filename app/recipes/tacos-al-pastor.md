# Beef Tacos al Pastor

Traditional Mexican tacos with marinated meat and grilled pineapple.

## Ingredients

- 600g pork shoulder, thinly sliced
- 1 fresh pineapple, sliced
- 8 flour tortillas
- 4 dried chilies
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tbsp vinegar
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp cumin
- Cilantro
- Onion, diced
- Lime wedges
- Salt

## Instructions

1. Rehydrate chilies and blend with garlic, vinegar, oil, and cumin
2. Marinate pork for at least 1 hour
3. Heat a pan or grill and cook pork until browned
4. Grill pineapple slices until caramelized
5. Warm tortillas
6. Fill with pork, top with pineapple
7. Garnish with cilantro and onion
8. Serve with lime wedges
