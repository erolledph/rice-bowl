# Satay - Indonesian Grilled Meat Skewers

Flavorful marinated meat skewers with creamy peanut sauce.

## Ingredients
- 600g chicken breast, cut into strips
- 3 tbsp soy sauce
- 2 tbsp lime juice
- 2 tbsp brown sugar
- 1 tsp turmeric
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- Wooden skewers, soaked

### Peanut Sauce:
- 1 cup peanut butter
- 1/2 cup coconut milk
- 3 tbsp soy sauce
- 2 tbsp lime juice
- 1 tbsp brown sugar
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tsp chili paste

## Instructions
1. Mix soy sauce, lime juice, sugar, turmeric, and garlic
2. Marinate chicken for 30 minutes
3. Thread chicken onto skewers
4. Grill or broil for 8-10 minutes, turning occasionally
5. Mix all peanut sauce ingredients
6. Serve skewers with peanut sauce

## Serving
Serve hot with rice and lime wedges.
