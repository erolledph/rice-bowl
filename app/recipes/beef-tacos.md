# Beef Tacos

Delicious Mexican street tacos with seasoned beef and fresh toppings .

## Ingredients

- 500g ground beef
- 8 taco shells or tortillas
- 1 onion, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tbsp taco seasoning
- 1 cup salsa
- Shredded lettuce
- Diced tomatoes
- Grated cheese
- Sour cream
- Lime wedges

## Instructions

1. Brown ground beef with onion and garlic
2. Add taco seasoning and water as per package instructions
3. Simmer for 5 minutes until thickened
4. Warm tortillas or shells
5. Fill each with beef mixture
6. Top with lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, and sour cream
7. Serve with salsa and lime wedges
