# Thai Green Curry Chicken

Aromatic and spicy Thai curry with tender chicken and fresh herbs.

## Ingredients

- 800g chicken breast, cubed
- 400ml coconut milk
- 3 tbsp green curry paste
- 200g green beans
- 1 cup Thai eggplant (optional)
- 3 kaffir lime leaves
- 2 tbsp fish sauce
- 1 tbsp palm sugar
- 200ml chicken broth
- Thai basil leaves
- Red chilies for garnish

## Instructions

1. Heat 100ml coconut milk in a pan
2. Add green curry paste and cook for 2 minutes
3. Add chicken and cook for 5 minutes
4. Pour in remaining coconut milk and broth
5. Add green beans and eggplant
6. Simmer for 10 minutes until chicken is cooked
7. Add fish sauce and palm sugar
8. Tear kaffir lime leaves and add to curry
9. Garnish with Thai basil and red chilies
10. Serve with jasmine rice
