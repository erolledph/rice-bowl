# Caesar Salad

Classic Caesar salad with homemade dressing and crispy croutons.

## Ingredients

- 1 head romaine lettuce, chopped
- 200g grilled chicken breast, sliced
- 50g Parmesan cheese, shaved
- 1 cup croutons
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 100ml mayonnaise
- 2 tbsp lemon juice
- 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
- Salt and pepper

## Instructions

1. Whisk together garlic, mayonnaise, lemon juice, and Worcestershire sauce
2. Season dressing with salt and pepper
3. Toss lettuce with dressing
4. Top with grilled chicken, croutons, and Parmesan
5. Serve immediately
