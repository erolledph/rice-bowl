# Chicken Biryani

Fragrant Indian rice dish layered with marinated chicken and spices.

## Ingredients

- 800g chicken breast, cubed
- 300g basmati rice
- 200g yogurt
- 3 tbsp biryani masala paste
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp ginger, minced
- 2 onions, sliced
- 4 green chilies
- Fresh cilantro and mint
- 4 tbsp ghee
- 2 bay leaves
- 4 cloves
- 1 tsp cumin seeds
- Salt

## Instructions

1. Marinate chicken in yogurt and biryani paste for 30 minutes
2. Cook rice with whole spices until 70% done
3. Fry onions until golden, set aside half
4. Heat ghee and cook marinated chicken for 10 minutes
5. In a heavy pot, layer rice and chicken
6. Top with fried onions, cilantro, and mint
7. Cover and cook on high heat for 3 minutes until steam forms
8. Lower heat and cook for 30 minutes
9. Turn off heat and let rest 10 minutes
10. Fluff with fork before serving
