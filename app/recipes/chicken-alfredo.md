# Chicken Alfredo

Creamy and indulgent pasta dish with tender chicken and Alfredo sauce.

## Ingredients

- 400g fettuccine pasta
- 300g chicken breast, sliced
- 200ml heavy cream
- 100g butter
- 100g Parmesan cheese, grated
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Salt and pepper
- Fresh parsley
- Nutmeg

## Instructions

1. Cook pasta according to package directions
2. In a pan, heat olive oil and cook chicken until golden, set aside
3. In same pan, melt butter and saut√© garlic
4. Add cream and bring to simmer
5. Stir in Parmesan cheese until melted
6. Add nutmeg, salt, and pepper
7. Return chicken to pan
8. Drain pasta and toss with Alfredo sauce
9. Garnish with fresh parsley
10. Serve immediately
