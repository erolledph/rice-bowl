# Beef Stir Fry

Tender beef with vegetables in a savory soy-ginger sauce

## Ingredients

- 500g beef sirloin, sliced thinly
- 3 cups mixed vegetables (broccoli, snap peas, carrots)
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp ginger, minced
- 4 tbsp soy sauce
- 2 tbsp sesame oil
- 1 tbsp cornstarch
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- Green onions
- Sesame seeds
- White pepper

## Instructions

1. Mix cornstarch with 2 tbsp water
2. Heat 1 tbsp oil and stir-fry beef until browned, set aside
3. Add remaining oil, stir-fry garlic and ginger for 30 seconds
4. Add vegetables and stir-fry for 4-5 minutes
5. Return beef to pan
6. Mix soy sauce, sesame oil, and cornstarch mixture
7. Pour sauce over beef and vegetables
8. Toss until coated and thickened (1-2 minutes)
9. Garnish with green onions and sesame seeds
10. Serve with rice
