# Vegetable Lasagna

Hearty and satisfying vegetable lasagna with layers of cheese and sauce.

## Ingredients

- 12 lasagna noodles
- 2 cups mixed vegetables (spinach, zucchini, mushrooms)
- 400g ricotta cheese
- 200g mozzarella cheese, shredded
- 100g Parmesan cheese, grated
- 1 egg
- 500ml tomato sauce
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Salt and pepper
- Italian herbs

## Instructions

1. Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions
2. Sauté vegetables with garlic in olive oil
3. Mix ricotta, egg, mozzarella, Parmesan, salt, and pepper
4. Spread 1/4 of tomato sauce on bottom of baking dish
5. Layer 3 noodles, 1/3 of cheese mixture, vegetables
6. Repeat layers, ending with sauce
7. Cover with foil and bake at 180°C for 40 minutes
8. Remove foil and bake 5 more minutes
9. Let rest 10 minutes before serving
