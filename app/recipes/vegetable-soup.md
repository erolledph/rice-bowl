# Vegetable Soup

Simple and nutritious vegetable soup perfect for any season.

## Ingredients

- 1.5 liters vegetable broth
- 2 carrots, diced
- 2 celery stalks, diced
- 1 onion, diced
- 1 potato, diced
- 1 cup green beans, chopped
- 1 can diced tomatoes
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- Bay leaf
- Thyme
- Salt and pepper

## Instructions

1. Heat olive oil and saut√© onion, carrot, and celery for 5 minutes
2. Add garlic and cook for 1 minute
3. Pour in vegetable broth and bring to boil
4. Add potato, green beans, and tomatoes
5. Add bay leaf and thyme
6. Simmer for 15 minutes until vegetables are tender
7. Season with salt and pepper
8. Remove bay leaf
9. Serve hot with crusty bread
