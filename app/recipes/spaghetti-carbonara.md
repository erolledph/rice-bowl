# Spaghetti Carbonara

Authentic Italian pasta dish with creamy sauce made from eggs and Parmesan.

## Ingredients

- 400g spaghetti
- 200g bacon, diced
- 4 eggs
- 100g Parmesan cheese, grated
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- Black pepper to taste
- Salt for pasta water

## Instructions

1. Cook spaghetti in boiling salted water until al dente
2. Fry bacon until crispy, add garlic and cook for 1 minute
3. Whisk eggs with Parmesan cheese and black pepper
4. Drain pasta, reserve 1 cup pasta water
5. Mix hot pasta with bacon, remove from heat
6. Add egg mixture and toss quickly, add pasta water if needed
7. Serve immediately with extra Parmesan
