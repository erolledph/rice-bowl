# Bibimbap - Korean Mixed Rice Bowl

A colorful and nutritious Korean rice bowl topped with vegetables, meat, and gochujang sauce.

## Ingredients
- 2 cups cooked rice
- 200g ground beef
- 2 cups spinach
- 1 cup carrots, julienned
- 1 cup zucchini, julienned
- 1 cup mushrooms, sliced
- 2 eggs
- 3 tbsp gochujang (Korean red chili paste)
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 2 cloves garlic, minced

## Instructions
1. Heat oil and stir-fry beef with garlic and 1 tbsp soy sauce until cooked
2. Blanch spinach and drain well
3. Stir-fry each vegetable separately with a pinch of salt
4. Fry eggs sunny-side up
5. Arrange rice in bowls, top with vegetables and beef
6. Mix gochujang with sesame oil and remaining soy sauce
7. Place fried egg on top and drizzle sauce

## Serving
Mix everything together before eating.
