# Grilled Salmon

Perfectly grilled salmon fillet with fresh lemon and aromatic herbs.

## Ingredients

- 2 salmon fillets (200g each)
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 2 lemons, sliced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- Fresh dill and thyme
- Salt and pepper
- Asparagus for serving

## Instructions

1. Preheat grill to medium-high heat
2. Brush salmon with olive oil, season with salt and pepper
3. Place lemon slices and herbs on top of each fillet
4. Grill for 6-8 minutes until cooked through
5. Serve with grilled asparagus and lemon wedges
