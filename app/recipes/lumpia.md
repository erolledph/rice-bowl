# Lumpia - Filipino Crispy Spring Rolls

Delightfully crispy spring rolls filled with savory meat and vegetables.

## Ingredients
- 16 lumpia wrappers
- 200g ground pork
- 1 cup cabbage, finely chopped
- 1/2 cup carrot, julienned
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 onion, finely chopped
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- Oil for frying
- Water for sealing

## Instructions
1. Heat oil and saut√© garlic and onion
2. Add ground pork and cook until done
3. Add cabbage and carrot, stir-fry for 3 minutes
4. Add soy sauce and let cool
5. Place filling on wrapper, roll tightly, seal edge with water
6. Deep fry in hot oil until golden brown
7. Drain on paper towels

## Serving
Serve hot with sweet and sour sauce or vinegar.
