# Vegetable Curry

Delicious Thai vegetable curry with aromatic spices and coconut milk.

## Ingredients

- 2 cups mixed vegetables (carrots, potatoes, bell pepper)
- 400ml coconut milk
- 3 tbsp curry paste
- 1 onion, diced
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp ginger, minced
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- 1 tbsp fish sauce
- 1 tbsp lime juice
- Fresh basil
- Salt

## Instructions

1. Heat oil and saut√© onion, garlic, and ginger
2. Add curry paste and cook for 2 minutes
3. Add vegetables and stir well
4. Pour in coconut milk
5. Simmer for 15-20 minutes until vegetables are tender
6. Add fish sauce and lime juice
7. Taste and adjust seasonings
8. Garnish with fresh basil
9. Serve with rice
