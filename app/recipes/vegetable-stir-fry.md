# Vegetable Stir Fry

Quick and healthy stir-fried vegetables with tofu in a savory sauce.

## Ingredients

- 2 cups mixed vegetables (broccoli, bell pepper, snap peas)
- 200g firm tofu, cubed
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp ginger, minced
- 3 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 1 tbsp cornstarch
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- Sesame seeds for garnish
- Green onions

## Instructions

1. Press tofu and cut into cubes
2. Heat oil in a wok or large pan
3. Stir-fry tofu until golden, set aside
4. Add more oil, stir-fry garlic and ginger for 30 seconds
5. Add vegetables and stir-fry for 5 minutes
6. Return tofu to pan
7. Mix soy sauce, sesame oil, and cornstarch with water
8. Pour sauce over and toss until thickened
9. Garnish with sesame seeds and green onions
