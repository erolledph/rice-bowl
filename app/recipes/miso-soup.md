# Miso Soup - Japanese Comfort Soup

A simple, healthy, and warming Japanese soup perfect for any meal.

## Ingredients
- 4 cups dashi broth
- 3 tbsp miso paste
- 150g tofu, cubed
- 1 sheet nori (seaweed), cut into strips
- 2 green onions, sliced
- Small handful of wakame seaweed

## Instructions
1. Heat dashi broth in a pot (don't boil)
2. Add tofu and wakame, heat gently
3. Dissolve miso paste in a small ladle with warm broth
4. Pour miso mixture into the pot and stir gently
5. Do not boil after adding miso
6. Add green onions just before serving

## Serving
Serve hot in bowls, topped with nori strips.
