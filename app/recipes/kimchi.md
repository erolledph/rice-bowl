# Kimchi - Korean Fermented Vegetables

Kimchi is a traditional Korean side dish made from fermented vegetables, primarily cabbage.

## Ingredients
- 1 kg napa cabbage
- 4 tbsp gochugaru (Korean red chili powder)
- 6 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp ginger, minced
- 3 tbsp fish sauce
- 2 tbsp salt
- 2 green onions, chopped

## Instructions
1. Cut cabbage into quarters and soak in salt water for 2 hours
2. Rinse thoroughly and drain
3. Mix gochugaru, garlic, ginger, fish sauce, and green onions
4. Apply mixture between cabbage leaves
5. Pack into jar and let ferment at room temperature for 3-5 days

## Notes
Serve chilled as a side dish with any Korean meal.
