# Filipino Chicken Adobo

Adobo is one of the most famous Filipino dishes - a savory and tangy stew.

## Ingredients
- 1 kg chicken, cut into pieces
- 1/2 cup soy sauce
- 1/4 cup vinegar
- 8 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 onion, sliced
- 4 bay leaves
- 1 tsp black pepper
- 2 tbsp oil
- 1 cup water

## Instructions
1. Heat oil in a pan and saut√© garlic and onion
2. Add chicken and brown on all sides
3. Add soy sauce, vinegar, bay leaves, and pepper
4. Add water and bring to boil
5. Lower heat and simmer for 30 minutes until chicken is tender

## Serving
Serve hot with steamed rice.
