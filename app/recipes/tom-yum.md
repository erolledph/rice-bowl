# Tom Yum Goong - Spicy Shrimp Soup

A iconic Thai soup known for its bold, aromatic flavors.

## Ingredients
- 1 liter chicken broth
- 400g shrimp
- 4 lemongrass stalks, bruised
- 3 galangal slices
- 4 kaffir lime leaves
- 3-5 Thai red chilies
- 3 tbsp fish sauce
- 4 tbsp lime juice
- 200g mushrooms, sliced
- 1 onion, sliced
- Cilantro for garnish

## Instructions
1. Bring broth to boil
2. Add lemongrass, galangal, lime leaves, and chilies
3. Simmer for 5 minutes
4. Add mushrooms and onion, simmer for 5 minutes
5. Add shrimp and cook for 3 minutes
6. Add fish sauce and lime juice
7. Taste and adjust seasonings

## Serving
Garnish with cilantro and serve hot.
