# Butter Chicken

Tender chicken in a rich, creamy tomato butter sauce

## Ingredients

- 800g chicken breast, cubed
- 100ml plain yogurt
- 400ml tomato puree
- 200ml cream
- 4 tbsp butter
- 2 onions, diced
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp ginger, minced
- 2 tbsp garam masala
- 1 tsp chili powder
- Fresh cilantro
- Salt

## Instructions

1. Marinate chicken in yogurt for 15 minutes
2. Melt butter and cook chicken until golden, set aside
3. In same pan, saut√© onions, garlic, and ginger
4. Add garam masala and chili powder, cook 1 minute
5. Add tomato puree and simmer for 10 minutes
6. Stir in cream and return chicken
7. Simmer for 10 more minutes until chicken is tender
8. Garnish with cilantro
9. Serve with rice or naan
