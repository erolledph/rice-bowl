# Pasta Primavera

Light Italian pasta dish loaded with fresh seasonal vegetables.

## Ingredients

- 400g penne pasta
- 2 cups mixed vegetables (zucchini, bell pepper, cherry tomatoes)
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 100ml olive oil
- 50g Parmesan cheese, grated
- Fresh basil
- Lemon juice
- Salt and pepper
- Red pepper flakes

## Instructions

1. Cook pasta according to package directions
2. In a large pan, heat olive oil and saut√© garlic
3. Add vegetables and cook for 5-7 minutes
4. Add pasta water (1 cup) to create a light sauce
5. Drain pasta and add to vegetables
6. Toss well, add lemon juice and red pepper flakes
7. Season with salt and pepper
8. Top with Parmesan and fresh basil
9. Serve immediately
